Check coolant, brake, power steering, and fuel. If you didn‚Äôt use fuel stabilizer, add an octane booster and fill up with premium gas.

5. Check Tires

Inflate to the recommended PSI and inspect for cracks or bulges.

Other Items

  • Inspect brakes, suspension, and exhaust.
  • Remove any material blocking the tailpipe.
  • Test headlights, blinkers, and taillights.
  • Inspect wipers and wash the car.
  • Air out the interior.

Once checks are complete, start your car and let it idle to operating temperature before driving. Take it easy the first few miles!
