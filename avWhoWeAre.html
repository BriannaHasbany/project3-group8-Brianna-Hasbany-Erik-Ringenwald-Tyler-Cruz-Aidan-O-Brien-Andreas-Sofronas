Our Mission

To ensure your satisfaction, whether you come for routine maintenance or major repairs. Word of mouth is our best salesman. We pride ourselves on building relationships with each of our clients. We value every customer who walks through the door, no matter how big or small the repair is.

At AV Repair, our only goal is to provide you with exceptional and honest repair work, without the inflated prices. We provide bumper-to-bumper service and use quality parts and fluids. We want you to feel confident that you will get professional service that never leaves you feeling cheated.
