Insured Collision Repairs

“No appraiser, insurer, or insurance representative shall refer a claimant to or away from any specific repair shop.”
– Commonwealth of MA Regulation 212 2.0.4

Your repair shop is your choice. Insurance companies cannot legally require you to use their preferred body shop or discourage you from choosing another. It’s your vehicle — you decide who repairs it.

Unfortunately, some insurance representatives use misleading tactics to cut costs. At AV Repair, we stand by your right to a safe, high-quality repair. We’ve been serving our community for over 25 years and work directly with your insurer to make the process hassle-free.

Your Rights in Massachusetts

  • Choose any licensed repair shop — not just one your insurer recommends.
  • Have your vehicle inspected at any location you select.
  • Get repairs that restore your car to its pre-accident condition.
  • Expect your insurer to negotiate in good faith.

Remember: Your Car, Your Choice. Choose AV Repair — your trusted, licensed collision repair shop.

Paint Services

AV Repair offers a full range of painting services — from small scratch repair to complete vehicle refinishing. Our specially trained technicians use an in-house paint mixing system to ensure every coating is applied with precision and care.

We use an online paint code retrieval system to match your vehicle’s exact color formula, mixing paint to fraction-of-an-ounce accuracy. This process reduces waste, protects the environment, and ensures you only pay for what’s used.

To achieve a flawless, factory-level finish, our team starts with high-quality primers applied to bare metal, then hand-sands and inspects every surface for imperfections. This careful preparation guarantees a smooth foundation for the sealer, color, and clear coats—restoring your vehicle’s original shine.

AV Repair uses manufacturer-specific build data and advanced diagnostic equipment to identify and correct structural damage according to factory specifications.

Our trained technicians restore your vehicle’s frame and suspension systems to pre-accident condition, ensuring proper alignment, safety, and stability on the road.

Rust Repair

For over 30 years, AV Repair has restored countless cars and trucks—often bringing vehicles back from “junkyard-ready” to roadworthy condition at a budget-friendly price.

Living in New England’s salty environment means rust is inevitable—from ocean spray to winter road salt. That’s why prevention and timely repair are key to protecting your vehicle’s frame, panels, and body.

Our experts specialize in removing rust buildup, repairing damage, and preventing future corrosion. Whether it’s a small spot or extensive rust, we restore your vehicle’s appearance and structural integrity.

Don’t wait for rust to spread—AV Repair’s technicians use proven techniques and quality materials to ensure your vehicle looks and performs its best for years to come.

Restoration

Restoring a classic car to period-correct condition comes with unique challenges. From vintage Cadillacs and Lincolns to Mercedes, VW, and Rolls Royce, projects often involve undoing decades of subpar repairs. Parts can be hard to find or no longer available—but AV Repair has the experience and resources to restore your vehicle to driver-quality or Concours condition.

We begin with a complete disassembly during frame-off restorations when needed, uncovering all potential problem areas. Our extensive network of suppliers allows us to track down rare parts, from emblems and hood ornaments to trim pieces. If parts can’t be found, we have the tools and expertise to fabricate or restore them, from chrome plating bumpers to repairing wheel spokes on a Model T.

While we specialize in complete restorations, we also offer stand-alone services such as metal work, custom paint, suspension repair, engine rebuilds, and electrical work. AV Repair can help get your classic back on the road, looking and performing as it was meant to.
